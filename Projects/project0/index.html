The 6 Continents of Andrew Curthoys

I have had the privilege of traveling to 6 of the 7 continents on Earth. Two of those continents - Europe and Asia - were more of a technicality for me, as they were both on a trip to Istanbul, where I was technically at the crossroads of Europe and Asia. I plan to make 2 trips to Europe this year, so I will have a more thorough Europe experience. I have places that I would like to visit in Asia as well, but have not made concrete plans just yet.
