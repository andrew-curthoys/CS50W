Africa

Not Much to See Here

My one trip to Africa was to Egypt for work. We have a Cairo office and in the fall of 2013 I took a trip with my team at the time for meetings with the team in Egypt. Unfortunately this coincided with the Arab Spring revolts, so security in the city was on high alert. We were unable to see the Pyramids and pretty much went from the hotel to the office and back every day we were there. Not a great trip but I can say I have been to Africa.
